Website Analytics Snapshot

April 2024 - April 2025

Since class has started, the website recorded a total of 42 new users and 22 active users, with an average engagement time of 55 seconds. Traffic peaked significantly in January, with the majority of users accessing the site directly. A small but meaningful portion came through referrals. The United States remained the primary source of traffic, with some international engagement from Belgium.

Top pages included "My Assignments," "My Portfolio," and the "Working with Images" project. Although traffic declined slightly over the spring months, the initial spike demonstrated strong interest, particularly when fresh content was available. Continuing to post updates and refining SEO strategies can help rebuild momentum and sustain long-term engagement. Overall, the site's foundational performance is strong and shows good potential for future growth.
